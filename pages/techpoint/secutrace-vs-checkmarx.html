Why Choose SecuTrace over Checkmarx?

True Air-Gapped Operation

SecuTrace runs entirely offline with no cloud dependency. Checkmarx requires cloud connectivity for updates and license validation, creating compliance gaps for regulated industries.

Built for Indian Compliance

Native support for SEBI CSCRF, RBI, CERT-In, and DPDP Act frameworks. Pre-built compliance reports that map directly to Indian regulatory requirements.

Competitive Pricing

Enterprise-grade SAST at a fraction of Checkmarx pricing. No hidden costs for air-gapped deployment or compliance modules that come standard with SecuTrace.
