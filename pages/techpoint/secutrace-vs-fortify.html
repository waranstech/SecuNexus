Why Choose SecuTrace over Fortify?

Fraction of the Cost

Fortify licensing is notoriously expensive with complex per-user and per-app models. SecuTrace offers transparent, competitive pricing with no hidden fees.

Indian Regulatory Compliance

Pre-built compliance mapping for SEBI CSCRF, RBI guidelines, and CERT-In advisories. Fortify lacks native Indian compliance frameworks entirely.

Seamless Offline Updates

Full offline rule and engine updates via secure transfer. Fortify's offline update process is cumbersome and often incomplete for air-gapped environments.
