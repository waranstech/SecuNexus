Common Pricing Questions

SecuNexa uses an annual license model based on the number of engines, projects, and users. Pricing is customized for each organization based on their specific requirements. There are no per-scan fees, no cloud hosting charges, and no surprise costs. Contact our sales team for a personalized quote.

Yes, absolutely. Many customers start with SecuTrace (SAST) or SecuLens (SCA) and expand to additional engines as their security program matures. Upgrading is seamless — additional engines integrate into the same unified dashboard and share the same vulnerability database.

We offer a 14-day proof-of-concept (PoC) for qualified organizations. This includes full platform access, installation on your infrastructure, scanning of up to 3 projects, and dedicated technical support. Contact sales to discuss eligibility.

All plans include onboarding support and access to documentation. Starter gets email support with 48-hour SLA. Professional adds phone support with 24-hour SLA. Enterprise includes a dedicated support engineer, 4-hour SLA for critical issues, and on-site support as needed.
