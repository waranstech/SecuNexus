What Your SCA Assessment Includes

Vulnerability Report

Every known CVE affecting your open-source components, with CVSS scores, exploit availability, and the exact dependency path that introduced each vulnerability.

License Compliance Report

Forensic license detection across all components with confidence scoring, copyleft propagation analysis, and license conflict identification between dependencies.

Full SBOM Export

Complete Software Bill of Materials in CycloneDX and SPDX formats listing every component, version, supplier, and dependency relationship.

Upgrade Path Analysis

Specific version upgrade recommendations for each vulnerable dependency, including breaking change warnings and transitive dependency impact analysis.

Dependency Health Score

Component age analysis, maintenance activity metrics, community health indicators, and end-of-life risk flags for every open-source dependency.

Regulatory Gap Analysis

Assessment of how your open-source posture maps to CERT-In, SEBI CSCRF, RBI CSF, and IRDAI requirements with specific gap identification.
