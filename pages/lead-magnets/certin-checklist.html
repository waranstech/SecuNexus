Checklist Preview

Here are 5 of the 21 items covered in the full checklist. Download the complete version to assess your entire CERT-In compliance posture.

1

6-Hour Incident Reporting Capability

Do you have a documented process and designated personnel to report cyber incidents to CERT-In within 6 hours of detection? Is the reporting channel (portal or email) tested quarterly?

2

180-Day Log Retention

Are all ICT system logs (firewall, IDS/IPS, application, authentication) retained for a minimum of 180 days within Indian jurisdiction with tamper-proof storage?

3

NTP Synchronization

Are all ICT systems synchronized to NTP servers provided by NIC or NPLI? Is clock drift monitored and alerts configured for synchronization failures?

4

Vulnerability Scanning & VAPT

Are regular vulnerability assessments and penetration testing (VAPT) conducted on all internet-facing and critical internal applications? Are findings remediated within defined SLAs?

5

Software Bill of Materials (SBOM)

Do you maintain a current SBOM for all production applications? Are open-source components monitored for newly disclosed vulnerabilities on an ongoing basis?
