Banking & Finance

Top-5 Indian Private Bank Achieves RBI CSF Compliance

Challenge

A leading private sector bank with 2,000+ applications needed to meet RBI Cyber Security Framework mandates for SSDLC and VAPT across all production applications. Their existing cloud-based SCA tool could not operate in the bank's air-gapped core banking environment, leaving critical systems unscanned.

Solution

Deployed SecuNexa's full platform (SecuTrace, SecuLens, SecuStrike) on-premises within the bank's isolated network. Integrated SAST and SCA into the bank's Jenkins-based CI/CD pipeline with automated quality gates. Established offline vulnerability database updates via secure data diode.

67%
Reduction in mean time to remediate

RBI CSF audit compliance

Government

Central Government Ministry Secures Citizen Services Portal

Challenge

A central government ministry operating a citizen services portal processing 10M+ transactions monthly needed to comply with CERT-In directives while operating in a classified air-gapped network. No application code could leave the secure facility for external scanning.

Solution

Deployed SecuNexa entirely within the ministry's secure data centre. Implemented SecuTrace for SAST scans on the portal's Java and Python codebase, SecuLens for open-source dependency analysis, and SecuShield for runtime protection of the production environment.

Results

340+
Critical vulnerabilities identified and fixed
6 hrs
CERT-In incident reporting SLA met
Zero
Security incidents post-deployment

Insurance

Major Insurance Provider Manages Open-Source Risk at Scale

Challenge

A top-10 Indian insurance company with 500+ microservices discovered during an IRDAI audit that they had no visibility into open-source components across their applications. License compliance was completely untracked, creating significant legal and regulatory exposure.

Solution

Rolled out SecuLens across all development teams with automated SBOM generation in CI/CD pipelines. Implemented forensic license detection to identify copyleft and license conflict risks. Created policy-as-code rules to block non-compliant dependencies before they enter production.

Results

12,400+
Open-source components inventoried
47
Copyleft license violations resolved
100%
SBOM coverage across production apps

Global IT Services Company Embeds Security in DevSecOps Pipeline
